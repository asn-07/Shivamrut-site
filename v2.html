Our 40-Year Legacy of Authentic Catering

Founded in 1983 in the heart of Dharwad region, Shivamrut Kitchen has been the trusted choice for authentic North Karnataka vegetarian catering across generations.

Our family recipes have been perfected over four decades, preserving the traditional flavors while adapting to modern presentation and service standards.

We understand that every function is unique, which is why we work closely with each client to create a customized menu that reflects their taste, budget, and cultural requirements.
