Our Legacy Since 1983

Generations of Culinary Excellence

Shivamrut Kitchen began its journey in 1983 in the Dharwad region of North Karnataka, with a passion for creating authentic vegetarian meals that celebrate our rich culinary heritage.

For over four decades, we've been the trusted choice for families and organizations across Karnataka, serving with dedication and maintaining the highest standards of taste and hygiene.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, perfected over time to deliver the authentic flavors of North Karnataka that our clients have come to love and trust.

Our Catering Services

Wedding Ceremonies

Complete catering for weddings of all scales, from intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations with hundreds of guests. Traditional North Karnataka wedding meals with all rituals observed.

Housewarming & Rituals

Graha Pravesh, Poojas, and other traditional rituals catered with authentic meals prepared according to custom. We understand the significance and requirements of each ritual.

Corporate Events

Catering for office parties, conferences, corporate gatherings, and school/college functions. Flexible menu options suitable for professional environments and large groups.

Personal Celebrations

Birthdays, anniversaries, naming ceremonies, baby showers, and other personal milestones celebrated with delicious food that makes your occasion memorable.

Small Kittu Parties

Intimate gatherings with friends and family catered with love and attention. Perfect for small groups who want authentic North Karnataka flavors in a cozy setting.

Customized Services

Tailored menus and services to meet your specific requirements. We work closely with you to create the perfect dining experience for your unique occasion.
