Our Story

Master Craftsmanship in Wooden Furniture Since 1993

City Furniture India brings decades of expertise in premium wooden furniture craftsmanship to your doorstep. Established with a vision to transform Indian homes with durable and elegant furniture solutions, we combine traditional woodworking artistry with contemporary design.

Our showroom showcases carefully curated collections of solid wood furniture, Magani designs, and premium steel home appliances. We source the finest quality teak wood, sheesham, and other premium woods to create pieces that stand the test of time and enhance your living spaces.

Each furniture item in our collection reflects meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail, offering both beauty and functionality for modern Indian homes.
